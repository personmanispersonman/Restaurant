You can compost too!

Composting is often misconcieved as being too difficult or time consuming to be worth it, but that is far from the truth. Composting can be done by anyone, without any special equipment necessary. If you have a small backyard, that is fine. Composting works fine at a small scale. Make a small bin to put your food scraps.
Special equipment is not required for composting. You do not need special composting bins, any bin will work fine. All you need is a bin to put your food scraps.
Many animals will not bother the compost, and the ones that do should be offset by the scraps you put in.
