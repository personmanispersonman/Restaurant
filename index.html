The Holy Grail of Juices

Juice Jesus is a restaurant based in Mississippi that specializes in smoothies. We order locally grown produce and use sustainable practices.

Our Goal

Our goal is for our restaurant to become sustainable using local farmers that use sustainable practices. We also will use a marketing strategy that keeps the community informed while also attracting them to our restaurant.

Sustainability

We are committed to delivering the best possible experience while simultaneously being very sustainable. We compost a large part of our unused food. The majority of our food comes from local farmers that use sustainable practices. Where we cannot find produce from sustainable farmers, we supplement with our own produce.
